Beliefs

We believe that music can transport people out of normal existence for a minute, show beauty, and bring inspiration. To us, music brings a sense of well-being, optimism, and comfort. We are so appreciative of these experiences, and we want to offer the best that we can make to the world.



Mission

Our mission is to collaborate and create with others and to increase the number of beautiful songs on the planet. Our goal is to craft documentary songs from people's experiences and spoken words.

We fulfill our mission by presenting live songwriting sessions and concerts. You can learn more about our songwriting method at the Documentary Songwriter site: docsong.org.



History

The Push Farther Project began as Alex Wilder's project for his degree from Oberlin College. He wanted to apply what he was learning about popular music. He decided to write a song, record a video, and try to earn one million hits on youtube. The finished product is the music video "Push Farther." As soon as we began working together, we knew that we liked playing together far too much to limit our potential to a one-song project. Since then, Push Farther has evolved into a collaborative vocal and instrumental trio, and we are excited to see what will come next.

 

Progress so far

We have crossed the one year mark as a band. Although we attend colleges in Oregon, Ohio, and upstate New York, we have managed to rehearse and write together through videoconferencing on Google Hangout. We have written 17 songs that document people's experiences and emotions. Over school breaks from school we have created and published an official music video. For our first song, Push Farther, we have earned 2,600 views from videos and live performances.


We know ourselves well enough to recognize that we tend to be intense individuals. We seek to learn how to pace ourselves and work in a sustainable way over the longer term. However, we are all confident that working together in the same room will produce awesome results. Thanks for reading about us and for supporting us.
